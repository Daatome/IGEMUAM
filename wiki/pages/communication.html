Social media content creation on Tiktok

Figure 4. iGEM UAM Tiktok channel

We believe that one of the best ways of making science dissemination is through brief interactive videos. That is why at iGEM UAM, we created videos about different scientific topics related to science in general, synthetic biology, and the iGEM Competition. The TikTok platform helped us reach more people than we had ever expected. It is important to be responsible with the knowledge we share. For that reason, we did bibliographic research before uploading a video. We hope that through the creation of this type of content, people all around the world with different backgrounds feel inspired about learning science.

Expert panel

As part of the dissemination of knowledge, on the last week of september, we presented the project sharing what was obtained in the social area, and what was raised in finance, process engineering and project engineering. Doctors León Sánchez García, Alejandro Morán Silva and Octavio Francisco González Castillo were fundamental pieces during the preparation and formulation period of the project, they have more than 15 years of experience formulating projects and companies from the engineering point of view.

In the social area, we address the problems that Mexico currently has in its crops due to the presence of pests that considerably reduce the quality of the product, leaving economic losses. As a solution, farmers use conventional agrochemicals, the principal disadvantage of its use is the erosion of soil, problems health and environmental pollution. In response to this problem, we propose the creation of a company that produces a biopesticide using synthetic biology techniques for its preparation.

Once the proposal was obtained, the financial part in costs and acquisitions to make the plant work was simulated, in process engineering, a flow chart was made, which describes each step of the manufacturing process and the material balances of each component through unit operations. Finally, the distribution diagrams of each area of ​​operation were drawn up with their respective staff of workers.

The presentation allowed us to obtain constructive criticism and opinions from the experts who introduced us to engineers from whom we will receive support to continue with the planning of the company.
