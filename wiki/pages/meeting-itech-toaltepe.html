Localization

Properly defining a production plant location is fundamental in developing a project. For this, it is essential to consider a variety of factors that allow for the correct localization.

To define the macro location, we selected five states of the republic and carried a further analysis on each one. This analysis considered the proximity to the agricultural sector of interest, availability of services, and industrial development in the region.

The states selected for this trial were: the State of México, Sonora, Coahuila, Jalisco, and Querétaro. Once we defined in which state of the republic to establish the plant, we proceeded to define the micro localization of the plant. For this, we select 3 municipalities in said state and repeat the analysis.

Macro Localization

The factors that were analysed in each tentative state are the demand for the product by potential customers, existing communications, the energy produced and alternative production methods, amount of water available, presence of industrial parks, accessibility to obtain raw material, the agility of tax processes for opening a business, transportation costs, available labour, and the security that the state has.

By weighting the aforementioned factors, a decision matrix was made. This matrix evaluates the advantages and disadvantages that each of the 5 selected states has. Said states are The State of Mexico, Jalisco, Querétaro, Sinaloa, and Coahuila

Each factor was assigned a weighting value based on its importance, whose total sum gave 1. In this case, the Demand was considered as the main factor since it implies reducing transportation costs and more direct distribution of the product with our market. On the other end, we selected safety as the last weighted facto.

Then, a score from 1 to 5 was assigned to each state according to the factor being evaluated, where 1=Very bad, 2=Bad, 3=Fair, 4=Good and 5=Very good. These values were multiplied by the weighting value, obtaining a Relative measurement. The relative values of each factor were added together to calculate the rating of each state, and therefore, defining which state is optimal for establishing the plant.

With this method, we decided that the best state to build the plant is the state of Jalisco as it scored the most of all states, with a rating of 3.89.

Micro Localization

For the micro-location analysis, we selected three municipalities in the state of Jalisco: Zapopan, Acatlán de Juárez, and Tlajomulco de Zúñiga.

The same way as the macro-localization analysis, we carried an extensive bibliographic search and evaluated the availability of each location to infrastructure and services, such as water, electricity, cost of land, cost of industrial buildings, security, and available industrial parks, among others. The method used to elaborate the matrix and calculate the relative ratings was the same used before.
