Product Analysis

Overview

As said before, our project plans the production of a biochemical biopesticide based on the spider venom peptides Omega-hexatoxina-Hv2a and U1-terafotoxina-Sp1a. The final product is intended to be a liquid concentrate with a 2% concentration of spider venom peptides divided evenly between each peptide. The objective of designing a liquid concentrate is for it to be used the same way that today’s agrochemicals are being used, providing a familiar and easy to use for local farmers.

Presentation

Based on our targeted market, medium and large producers, we needed a standard and easily to use presentation. Considering that the performance of our biopesticide is comparable with the similar products available, we estimate that for the treatment of 1 hectare of crops will require the application of 5 litters of SPIDICDE-Cx (Vestaron, 2021 & Bayer, 2021). Therefore, a reasonable presentation would be for us to produce 5 litters bottles, given the fact that medium producers often own 5 hectares of farm or more and thus needing only 5 bottles of SPIDICIDE-Cx.
